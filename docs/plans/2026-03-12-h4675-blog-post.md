**Style rules (MUST follow):**
- Contractions always (don't, can't, won't)
- Short paragraphs (1-3 sentences max)
- NO em dashes ever (use commas, colons, parentheses, periods)
- Physical verbs ("bolted on," "stripped back," not "added," "simplified")
- Bold sparingly (1-2 per section)
- Numbers as digits
- No banned phrases: "delve," "landscape," "game-changer," "straightforward," "furthermore," "additionally," etc.
- FATAL: No "This isn't X. This is Y." or any negation-then-assertion pattern
